## 🚀 Welcome to PeerlyPay

PeerlyPay is a decentralized P2P fiat-to-crypto marketplace designed to let you easily trade USDC for local fiat (and vice versa) without relying on centralized exchanges. 

We built this for **remote workers, freelancers, and digital nomads** in emerging markets who earn in crypto but have real-world, local expenses. Skip the KYC-heavy platforms and risky OTC deals—PeerlyPay secures your funds using a trustless smart contract escrow.

## ✨ What You Can Do

* **Trade USDC ↔ Fiat Seamlessly**: Access a global P2P marketplace tailored for local spending.
* **Use Your Favorite Local Payment Methods**: We support Bank Transfers, MercadoPago, Nequi, Pago Móvil, Zelle, Wise, and more.
* **Chat Securely**: Coordinate payment details directly with your counterparty using our built-in, secure chat.
* **Trade with Confidence**: Funds are locked in a non-custodial escrow. If a disagreement happens, our cross-chain dispute resolution steps in to ensure fairness.
* **Manage Orders in Real-Time**: A beautiful, mobile-first interface to track your active trades, completed history, and escrow steps.

## 🔄 How It Works

Trading on PeerlyPay is simple and secure:

1. **Connect & Match** 🤝 
   Connect your wallet and either create a new offer or match with an existing one on the marketplace. USDC is immediately locked safely in our escrow.
2. **Transfer Fiat** 💸
   The buyer sends the fiat directly to the seller using the agreed-upon local payment method (e.g., Bank Transfer, MercadoPago).
3. **Confirm & Release** ✅
   Once the seller confirms they have received the fiat, the smart contract instantly releases the USDC to the buyer.
4. **Dispute Protection** 🛡️
   If something goes wrong, a decentralized dispute resolution process handles the arbitration to ensure funds go to the rightful owner.
